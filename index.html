Welcome to 3 XO

This is a simple game of Tic-Tac-Toe. The game is played on a 3x3 grid where two players take turns to place their mark (either X or O) on an empty cell. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

However, there is a twist in this game. Each player has a limited number of symbols to place on the board (either X or O). Each player has 3 X's or 3 O's to place on the board. When a player places all 3 of their symbols on the board, the oldest symbol is removed and the player can place a new symbol on the board. The oldest symbol that will be removed is marked with a darker color and an asterisk (*).

In this variant of Tic-Tac-Toe, there are no draws. The game will continue until one player wins. So, you need to concentrate and choose your moves wisely!

Good luck and have fun!
